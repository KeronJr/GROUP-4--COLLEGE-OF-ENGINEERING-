Why Choose the College of Engineering?

The College of Engineering is dedicated to excellence in engineering education, research, and service. Here's why you should choose us:

Our faculty members are renowned experts in their fields, providing students with unparalleled mentorship and guidance. Through state-of-the-art facilities and a dynamic curriculum, students engage in real-world projects and gain practical skills that are in high demand in the engineering industry. Our collaborative and interdisciplinary approach fosters a culture of innovation and creativity, preparing our graduates to excel in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

General Information

The College of Engineering at is dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and academic excellence. With a rich history of academic distinction, our institution has been at the forefront of engineering education since its establishment in 1988. We pride ourselves on our commitment to providing a comprehensive learning environment that nurtures the intellectual growth and development of our students. Our diverse faculty of 10 esteemed members is dedicated to shaping the next generation of engineering leaders through their expertise and mentorship.
